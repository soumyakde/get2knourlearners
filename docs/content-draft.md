## 2. How It Works

**Section header:** From Content to Composite Index — Automatically

Four steps, shown as a simple horizontal flow:

1. **Deliver** — Learners move through your content in sequence, answering built-in checks for understanding as they go.
2. **Capture** — Each learner responds to validated instruments measuring not just what they know, but how engaged, motivated, and personally connected they feel to the material — plus short open-ended reflections in their own words.
3. **Analyze** — The platform automatically scores assessments, runs the statistics researchers would normally run by hand (t-tests, ANOVA, repeated-measures ANOVA), and uses AI to code open-ended reflections against your own themes or constructs.
4. **Understand** — Every learner gets a composite index blending what they learned with how they engaged and why — delivered instantly, with guided, cited interpretation so you know what the numbers actually mean.

**Callout:** No spreadsheets. No statistical software. No separate qualitative coding tool. One platform, one click.

## 3. The Science

**Section header:** Built on Validated Research, Not Guesswork

**Intro paragraph:**
Assessment frameworks are never neutral — they embody a particular view of what learning is and how it should be evidenced. Get2KnoUrLearners is built on a specific, defensible view: that learning is an active process of constructing understanding (Piaget; Vygotsky), that it unfolds across cognitive, affective, and behavioral dimensions at once (Krathwohl et al.), and that quantitative and qualitative evidence, placed in dialogue, reveal more than either could alone (Creswell & Plano Clark).

**Sub-section: The instruments**

- **Conceptual engagement** — measured with a scale grounded in conceptual change theory, assessing whether learners find instructional content coherent, credible, plausible, and personally relevant (Heddy et al., 2018) — the conditions research shows are necessary for deep engagement with new ideas.
- **Situational engagement** — measured with a scale capturing engagement, effort, and flow as they actually occur within a specific activity, not as a vague retrospective impression (Rotgans & Schmidt, 2011).
- **Situational motivation** — measured with a scale grounded in Self-Determination Theory, distinguishing intrinsic interest from external pressure — because *why* a learner engages matters as much as *whether* they do (Guay et al., 2000).

**Sub-section: Rigor under the hood**
Every composite score is built on the same psychometric standards a peer-reviewed study would use — construct reliability, Item Response Theory for item-level quality, and transparent, versioned scoring — so results hold up to scrutiny, not just intuition.

**Callout, smaller text:** Every instrument is fully swappable — bring your own validated scale, or use ours.

## 4. Features

**Section header:** Everything a Non-Technical Educator Needs — Nothing They Don't

Grid of feature cards:

- **One-click AI qualitative analysis** — reflections coded automatically against your themes, with every theme traceable back to exactly who said what
- **Automatic statistics** — means, medians, histograms, box plots, t-tests, ANOVA, and repeated-measures ANOVA, generated and explained without a stats course
- **Composite Competency Index** — one number per learner blending content knowledge with engagement and motivation, longitudinally, over the full course
- **Fully customizable instruments** — every assessment and survey lives in a plain text file, editable in minutes by anyone comfortable with a word processor — no developer required
- **Cohort and individual views** — drill from a class-wide trend down to one learner's specific answer, instantly
- **Export anytime** — pull any slice of the data to CSV for your own further analysis, no lock-in
- **Multiple AI models, your choice** — pick the LLM provider you trust, or run more than one and measure their agreement

## 6. For Researchers & Partners

**Section header:** Where This Came From

**Body copy:**
Get2KnoUrLearners started as a solution to a real research problem: existing survey and learning platforms couldn't support the sequential, module-linked, mixed-methods data collection a rigorous longitudinal study required. Rather than force a study design to fit an off-the-shelf tool, the platform was built from the ground up around what evidence-based learning research actually needs.

That origin matters. It means every design decision — the instrument choices, the scoring transparency, the audit trail — was made under the same standards a peer-reviewed study is held to, not retrofitted after the fact for a product pitch.

**Sub-section: The longer vision**
The goal isn't a one-time assessment tool — it's a sustainable ecosystem for ongoing, high-integrity education research. Used longitudinally, the platform can help build a per-learner picture that evolves over time: not just what a student knows today, but how their interests, engagement, and motivation are developing — informative for everything from classroom instruction to long-term career-interest tracking. Democratizing this kind of evaluation, so any educator or program leader can access research-grade insight without a research team, is the underlying goal.
